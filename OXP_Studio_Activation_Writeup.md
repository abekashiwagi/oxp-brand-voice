# OXP Studio Activation & Usage Report

**Date:** March 30, 2026
**Reporting Period:** March 23 – March 30, 2026 (dashboard data); March 24 – April 10, 2026 (client activation calls)

---

## Executive Summary

In the first week of instrumented data (Mar 23–30), **11 unique client accounts** used L2 agents, generating **162 completed agent executions** across **27 distinct agent types**. Over **2,800 agent invocations** were requested during the same period across **70+ agent types**, spanning workflows from leasing and applications to financial operations and vendor management.

On the activation side, **31 unique client calls** have been requested via Calendly since March 24 from **~29 unique client organizations**, with requests extending through April 10 over a ~2.5-week window.
